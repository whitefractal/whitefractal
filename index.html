About Us

White Fractal are an independent software engineering consultancy based in Oxford, UK. We work for tech start-ups, spin-outs, and R&D groups, particularly those with an emphasis on science or mathematics. We bring a wealth of experience in every stage of product development to offer advice, insight, and technical strategy as well as hands-on software development.

Big pictures have infinite detail

Software products are like fractals - there's detail at every zoom level. We provide big-picture thinking for CTOs and Heads-of, zooming in from your commercial value-chain all the way to the finest details of your software — and everything in between.

Consulting For

Start-ups • Spin-outs • R&D

Building a new technical product and the organisation to support it presents a number of unique challenges. White Fractal can help your organisation meet these challenges in either of two ways: Reports & Analysis or Hands-on Engineering.

Reports & Analysis

Depending on your needs we can gather information from using your product, from your documentation, from your source code, from you infrastructure & build processes, and from one-to-one discussions with your staff of all levels. We will then bring our findings together to provide clarity and insight to guide your technical strategy, in the form of a written report and/or a presentation. We offer reports to fulfil a number of different objectives:

  • Fresh Eyes An external validation of your product, your codebase, your engineering proceses, or your technical strategy.
  • From prototype to MVP Guidance on building your internal prototype into a Minimum Viable Product and start onboarding customers.
  • From MVP to operation Strategy for expanding and backfilling your MVP, and the supporting organisation, into a robust product.
  • Freeing up innovators A standard problem is how your research team can hand over responsibility for Product 1.0 to start and work to innovate on 1.1 or even 2.0.
  • Value analysis Not all parts of a codebase are of equal value, and knowing which parts to 'work smarter' on is key to efficient growth.
  • Zoom-out pivots Re-engineering in response to changes in commercial strategy without breaking anything.
  • The Difficult Second Product Guidance on leveraging your existing assets to develop another fantastic product.
  • Bespoke reports We are flexible and can fit your needs.
