About Us

White Fractal are an independent software engineering consultancy based in Oxford, UK. We work for tech start-ups, spin-outs, and R&D groups, particularly those with an emphasis on science or mathematics. We bring a wealth of experience in every stage of product development to offer advice, insight, and technical strategy as well as hands-on software development.

Big pictures have infinite detail

We believe software products are like fractals - there's crucial detail at every zoom level. We specialise in providing big-picture thinking for CTOs and Heads-of, zooming from your commercial value-chain all the way into the finest details of your software — and everything in between.

Consulting For

Start-ups • Spin-outs • R&D

  • Academic research
  • Idea development
  • Prototyping & Proof of Concept
  • Minimum Viable Product
  • Productionising & Operationalising
  • Scaling Up
  • Second Product
